2023 Taipei 101 Guide:

Taipei 101 is a famous place in Taiwan. Most tourists visit this palce at their first time.

There are 2 ways to go Taipei 101:

Route 1
Take the Taipei MRT, get off at the Taipei World Trade Center Station (Taipei 101 Station).

Route 2
If you are in a group, your travel agent may arrange this place.

Entrance fee is $600 NTD; service not include the photo or the souvenir. Your group will get off the tour bus in the Shin-yi Road.

Group entrance is at 5th floor.

When you leave the top platform, take an elevtor to the 5th floor. Then tourists get together at the Shin-yi Road (south side) to wait for the tour bus.
