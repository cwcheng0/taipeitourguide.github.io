2023 Ximending Guide:

Ximending is an area. If you take MRT, get off at the Ximen Station. Exit is #6. You may spend at least 2 hours in this place.
Ximending can shop cheap products, such as pearl milk, sneakers and have fun. We recommend a place called "Red Envelop". It is a place to listen to old songs, such as mandarin, Taiwanese and English. There were more than a dozen places in this area before COVID-19; however, there is only few left nowadays. We recommend this place "Phoenix." Entry fee is $300 NTD. you may ask for a few empty red envelops for free. You can give $100 into a red envelop to give it to the singer. Sometimes they will prepare olf English songs for you.
