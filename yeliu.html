2023 Yeliu Guide:

The only way to go Yeliu is by bus.

There are 3 methods to go Yeliu:

Route 1
Crown Northern Coast Shuttle Bus #716


Route 2
Keelung Bus #953


Route 3
Take a minibus, contact a travel agent to hire a direct bus.

A mini bus can take 20 people; usually min is 6 persons.

Price: $12,000 ~ $15,000 NTD/bus

Service: a tour guide + (travel agent)insurance + Notice

Not included: lunch
