2023 Hwa Shan 1914 Creative Park:

Young people like to visit this place.

There are 2 ways to visit Huashan 1914 Creative Park:

Route 1
Take the Taipei MRT; get off at the Shan Dao Temple Station. Continue to walk around 7 minutes.

Route 2
Group tour bus

Route 3
Take a minibus, contact a travel agent to hire a bus.

A mini bus can take 20 people; usually min is 6 persons.

Price: $12,000 ~ $15,000 NTD/bus

Service: a tour guide + (travel agent)insurance + Notice

Not included: lunch

Tourists visit this place in the afternoon and at night. There are various shows in this place. You can see the old Taiwan style life.
