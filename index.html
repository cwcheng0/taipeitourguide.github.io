TAIPEI

We provide tour guide training, tour guide group information.

Do I need a tour guide in Taiwan?

In Taiwan, it is easy to find a person to ask where to go; however, if you come to Taiwan in a group, you may need a tour guide to help you.

How to find a tour guide?

Please tell your needs to us; travel agents can find tour guide from us, too.

Places in Taipei

You may hear some interesting places in Taipei or Taiwan. You may get to these places by bus, MRT or just walk. In Taipei City, the government bureau set up a few web sites to provide foreigners tourism information. It is easy to find the tips from web search. Volunteered people in many tourist information places also warmly provide good advises to you.

However, just a few tricks may save your time and money. Please read our web site and get these information.

Our Tour Guides

We are all licensed tour guides. Previously we are all Taipei Tour Guide Association members. Since our association does not maintain a web page any more, we decided to host a web page to serve previous and upcoming tourists.

We are all experienced tour guide for more than 10 years. We understand your needs very well.

Best Possible Way To Reach

we can be reached by booking. We are working hard to upgrade our web page to see if we can let you book immediately and serve you in 1 hour. Currently we still need your help to provide us enough information. We may advise you some tips in advance or we can well you in person.
